The Gibson ES-335 in Sixties Cherry is a classic semi-hollowbody electric guitar that pays homage to the iconic designs of the 1960s. Renowned for its versatile tone, comfortable playability, and timeless aesthetics, the ES-335 remains a staple in the world of electric guitars.


Key Features:



Semi-Hollowbody Construction:

The ES-335 features a semi-hollowbody design, combining the warmth of a hollowbody with the sustain and feedback resistance of a solidbody. This construction provides a balanced and resonant tone, making it suitable for various musical genres.


Maple Body with Poplar Center Block:

Crafted with a maple body and a poplar center block, the guitar achieves a harmonious balance between sustain and feedback control. The combination of these tonewoods contributes to the ES-335's distinctive sound and responsiveness.


Sixties Cherry Finish:

The Sixties Cherry finish adds a touch of vintage charm to the ES-335, paying homage to the classic aesthetics of the 1960s. The rich red hue enhances the guitar's visual appeal, capturing the essence of a bygone era.


Slim C-Shaped Neck Profile:

The slim C-shaped neck profile offers a comfortable and fast-playing experience. Whether you're playing intricate jazz chords or bending notes in blues solos, the neck profile allows for easy navigation across the fretboard.


Rosewood Fingerboard:

The rosewood fingerboard adds warmth and smoothness to the guitar's tonal character. With classic dot inlays, the fingerboard provides a familiar and visually appealing playing surface.


Dual '57 Classic Humbucking Pickups:

Equipped with dual '57 Classic humbucking pickups, the ES-335 delivers a vintage-inspired, PAF-style tone. These pickups offer a blend of warmth, clarity, and dynamic response, making them well-suited for a range of musical styles.


Handwired Control Assembly:

The handwired control assembly ensures reliable and responsive tone shaping. With individual volume and tone controls for each pickup, players have the flexibility to sculpt their sound to perfection.


Historic Nickel Hardware:

The historic nickel hardware, including the Tune-O-Matic bridge and stopbar tailpiece, enhances the guitar's vintage aesthetics. This hardware not only contributes to the guitar's classic look but also ensures stability and sustain.


Quality Craftsmanship:

Built with Gibson's renowned craftsmanship, the ES-335 in Sixties Cherry reflects meticulous attention to detail. Every component is carefully selected and assembled to meet the high standards associated with the Gibson name.


Visual Appeal:

With its Sixties Cherry finish, classic body shape, and vintage appointments, the Gibson ES-335 is a visually striking instrument. It captures the essence of a timeless era while offering modern playability.


Conclusion:

The Gibson ES-335 in Sixties Cherry is a versatile and iconic guitar that brings together vintage aesthetics and modern playability. Whether you're playing jazz, blues, rock, or beyond, this guitar remains a symbol of timeless tone and style.
