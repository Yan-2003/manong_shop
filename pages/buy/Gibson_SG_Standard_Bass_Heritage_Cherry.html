The Gibson SG Standard Bass in Heritage Cherry is a classic bass guitar that inherits the iconic design of the legendary SG series. Known for its slim body, distinctive double-cutaway shape, and powerful tones, this bass is a testament to Gibson's rich heritage and commitment to quality craftsmanship.


Key Features:



Classic SG Design:

The SG Standard Bass features the timeless double-cutaway design of the SG series, offering a comfortable and lightweight body with easy access to the higher frets. The heritage cherry finish adds a touch of vintage elegance to the instrument.


Short Scale Length:

With a short scale length, the SG Standard Bass provides a slinkier feel and is well-suited for players who prefer a shorter reach between frets. This characteristic contributes to a unique playing experience and distinctive tonal characteristics.


Classic Humbucking Pickups:

Equipped with classic humbucking pickups, the bass delivers a powerful and articulate sound. The dual pickups capture the essence of the SG's signature tones, providing a versatile sonic palette suitable for various playing styles.


Solid Mahogany Body and Neck:

Constructed with a solid mahogany body and neck, the SG Standard Bass ensures a warm and resonant tone. The mahogany wood contributes to the bass's sustain and depth, making it a great choice for rock, blues, and other genres.


Rosewood Fingerboard:

The rosewood fingerboard adds a smooth and warm feel to the instrument, enhancing playability and providing a comfortable surface for fretting. The classic dot inlays contribute to the SG's traditional aesthetic.


Adjustable Bridge and Tailpiece:

Featuring an adjustable bridge and tailpiece, the SG Standard Bass allows for precise intonation adjustments and optimal setup preferences. This ensures accurate tuning and a customized playing experience.


Classic Control Layout:

The classic control layout includes volume and tone knobs for each pickup, offering straightforward tonal shaping options. This simplicity allows players to dial in their preferred sound with ease.


Quality Craftsmanship:

Built with Gibson's commitment to quality craftsmanship, the SG Standard Bass in Heritage Cherry exemplifies attention to detail and durability. The careful selection of materials and construction techniques ensures a reliable and long-lasting instrument.


Visual Appeal:

The Heritage Cherry finish, combined with the SG's sleek and angular design, gives the bass a classic and visually appealing look. It's a statement instrument on stage or in the studio.


Conclusion:

The Gibson SG Standard Bass in Heritage Cherry is a timeless instrument that pays homage to the legacy of the SG series. With its classic design, powerful pickups, and quality construction, it stands as a versatile and reliable choice for bassists seeking both style and substance.
