The Ibanez TOD10 Tim Henson Signature Electric Guitar is a cutting-edge instrument designed in collaboration with Tim Henson, the talented guitarist from the progressive metal band Polyphia. This signature model combines striking aesthetics, innovative features, and exceptional playability to cater to the needs of modern players.


Key Features:



Unique Tree Of Life Inlay:

The guitar boasts a distinctive "Tree Of Life" fretboard inlay, showcasing Tim Henson's artistic vision. This intricate inlay design adds a touch of elegance and sets the TOD10 apart with its unique aesthetic appeal.


Flamed Maple Top:

Featuring a flamed maple top, the TOD10 offers a visually stunning appearance with beautiful wood grain patterns. The flamed maple not only enhances the guitar's visual appeal but also contributes to its tonal characteristics.


Mahogany Body:

The mahogany body provides a solid and resonant foundation, delivering a warm and well-balanced tone. This tonewood choice is well-suited for a variety of playing styles and genres.


Wizard III Roasted Maple Neck:

The Wizard III Roasted Maple neck offers a slim and fast-playing profile. Roasted maple provides added stability, increased resonance, and a smooth playing experience, making it ideal for virtuosic playing styles.


DiMarzio® Fusion Edge Pickups:

Equipped with DiMarzio® Fusion Edge pickups, the TOD10 delivers a high-output and articulate sound. These pickups are designed to capture the nuances of modern playing styles, making them perfect for the demands of progressive metal and rock.


Floating Mono-Rail Bridge:

The floating Mono-Rail bridge enhances string isolation and sustain. This design choice allows each string to vibrate independently, minimizing crosstalk and ensuring clarity, especially during fast and intricate playing.


Gotoh® MG-T Locking Machine Heads:

The Gotoh® MG-T locking machine heads provide reliable tuning stability and quick string changes. The locking mechanism ensures precise tuning, even with aggressive playing and tremolo use.


Killswitch and Coil-Tap Functionality:

Featuring a killswitch and coil-tap functionality, the TOD10 offers additional sonic options. The killswitch allows for stutter effects, while the coil-tap function provides a single-coil tone for added tonal versatility.


Visual Aesthetics:

With its unique fretboard inlay, flamed maple top, and modern features, the Ibanez TOD10 Tim Henson Signature Electric Guitar is visually stunning. It captures Tim Henson's artistic expression and stands out as a signature model that blends style with substance.


Conclusion:

For players seeking a signature guitar that combines aesthetics with cutting-edge features, the Ibanez TOD10 Tim Henson Signature Electric Guitar is a standout choice. Whether you're a fan of Tim Henson's playing style or looking for a high-performance instrument, the TOD10 is designed to inspire and elevate your musical expression.
