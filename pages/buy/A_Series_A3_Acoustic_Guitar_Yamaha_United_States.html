The Yamaha A Series A3 Acoustic Guitar represents a harmonious blend of premium craftsmanship, cutting-edge technology, and rich tonal characteristics. Designed for discerning acoustic players, this guitar embodies Yamaha's commitment to quality and innovation.


Key Features:



Solid Sitka Spruce Top:

The A3 features a solid Sitka spruce top, known for its responsiveness and resonance. This tonewood choice contributes to a bright and articulate sound with a broad dynamic range, making it suitable for various playing styles.


Solid Rosewood Back and Sides:

The solid rosewood back and sides enhance the guitar's warmth and depth. Rosewood adds a lush and resonant character to the tone, making the A3 well-suited for both fingerstyle and strumming techniques.


Scalloped Bracing:

Utilizing scalloped bracing, the A3 optimizes the balance between strength and flexibility, allowing the top to vibrate more freely. This design choice contributes to a more nuanced and responsive instrument.


High-Comfort Neck Profile:

The high-comfort neck profile ensures a smooth and ergonomic playing experience. Whether you're playing intricate fingerstyle passages or navigating up and down the neck, the A3 provides comfort and playability.


SRT2 Pickup System:

Equipped with Yamaha's SRT2 (Studio Response Technology 2) pickup system, the A3 delivers an authentic and natural acoustic sound when amplified. This advanced system captures the nuances of the guitar's tone, providing a high-quality plugged-in experience.


Onboard Controls:

The onboard controls for the SRT2 pickup system allow for easy adjustments to the guitar's tone and volume when connected to an amplifier or sound system. This ensures versatility and control in various performance settings.


Ebony Fingerboard and Bridge:

The ebony fingerboard and bridge not only contribute to the guitar's aesthetic appeal but also enhance the clarity and sustain of each note. Ebony is a durable and sonically rich material that complements the overall tonal profile of the A3.


Gloss Finish:

The gloss finish adds a touch of elegance to the guitar's appearance while providing protection against wear and tear. The finish enhances the wood's natural beauty and ensures long-lasting durability.


Quality Craftsmanship:

Built with Yamaha's meticulous craftsmanship, the A Series A3 reflects the brand's dedication to quality. Every detail, from the choice of tonewoods to the finishing touches, is crafted with precision and care.


Visual Appeal:

With its classic dreadnought body shape, attractive tonewoods, and attention to detail, the Yamaha A Series A3 Acoustic Guitar is visually appealing. It's a stunning instrument that looks as good as it sounds.


Conclusion:

For acoustic guitarists seeking a high-quality instrument with a perfect balance of tradition and innovation, the Yamaha A Series A3 Acoustic Guitar stands as a testament to Yamaha's legacy of producing exceptional guitars that inspire musicians around the world.
