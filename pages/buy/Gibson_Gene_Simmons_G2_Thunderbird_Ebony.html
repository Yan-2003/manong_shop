The Gibson Gene Simmons G2 Thunderbird Ebony is a signature bass guitar designed in collaboration with the legendary bassist Gene Simmons of KISS. This instrument not only pays homage to the iconic Thunderbird design but also incorporates features tailored to Gene Simmons' playing style and preferences.


Key Features:



Signature Thunderbird Design:

Featuring the unmistakable Thunderbird body design, the G2 Thunderbird Ebony pays tribute to the classic aesthetic that has become synonymous with Gene Simmons' stage presence. The unique body shape provides a bold and striking visual identity.


Ebony Finish:

The bass is finished in ebony, adding a touch of elegance and a sleek, dark aesthetic. The ebony finish not only enhances the instrument's visual appeal but also complements its rock and metal-oriented character.


Active Electronics:

Equipped with active electronics, the G2 Thunderbird offers a powerful and dynamic tonal range. The active circuitry allows for precise control over the bass and treble frequencies, catering to the needs of modern rock and metal genres.


Dual Humbucking Pickups:

The bass is fitted with dual humbucking pickups, delivering a high-output sound with a punchy low end and clear highs. This setup is well-suited for achieving the distinctive growl and sustain associated with the Thunderbird bass.


Gene Simmons Signature Inlays:

Featuring signature inlays on the fretboard, the bass showcases Gene Simmons' personal touch. These inlays add a custom element to the instrument and serve as a nod to the iconic status of the KISS bassist.


Solid Mahogany Body:

The bass is crafted with a solid mahogany body, contributing to a rich and resonant tone. The mahogany construction enhances sustain and ensures a robust foundation for the powerful sound associated with the Thunderbird series.


Three-Point Adjustable Bridge:

The three-point adjustable bridge provides stability and allows for precise intonation adjustments. This feature is crucial for achieving optimal playability and tuning accuracy, especially during energetic live performances.


Set Neck Construction:

With a set neck construction, the G2 Thunderbird offers enhanced sustain and access to higher frets. The seamless connection between the neck and body contributes to the bass's overall resonance and playability.


Visual Presence:

Designed to command attention on stage, the Gibson Gene Simmons G2 Thunderbird Ebony not only delivers a powerful sound but also boasts a visual presence that reflects the iconic style of Gene Simmons.


Conclusion:

For fans of Gene Simmons and players looking to capture the essence of KISS's legendary bassist, the Gibson Gene Simmons G2 Thunderbird Ebony offers a combination of striking aesthetics, powerful electronics, and quality construction, making it a standout instrument in the Thunderbird lineup.
