The Fender American Professional Jazz Bass is a pinnacle of craftsmanship and tonal excellence, designed to meet the demands of professional bassists. Renowned for its versatility, playability, and iconic design, this bass guitar is a flagship model in Fender's lineup.


Key Features:



Classic Jazz Bass Design:

Staying true to the classic Jazz Bass design, the American Professional model features the distinct offset waist body shape, delivering a comfortable playing experience and a timeless aesthetic.


V-Mod Pickups:

Equipped with Fender's V-Mod pickups, the Jazz Bass delivers a rich and articulate tone. These pickups are crafted with a blend of alnico magnet types, providing a balanced and versatile sound suitable for various musical genres.


Slim "C"-Shaped Neck:

The slim "C"-shaped neck profile enhances playability, allowing for smooth and comfortable fretting. It's an ideal choice for bassists who value effortless navigation across the fretboard.


HiMass Vintage Bridge:

The HiMass Vintage bridge enhances sustain and stability, contributing to a solid and resonant tone. The combination of modern construction and vintage aesthetics makes it a reliable and performance-oriented bass.


Posiflex Graphite Support Rods:

Featuring Posiflex graphite support rods in the neck, the bass provides additional stability and reinforcement. This ensures consistent playability and helps resist changes in neck curvature due to environmental factors.


Narrow-Tall Frets:

The narrow-tall frets promote precise intonation and facilitate bending, giving bassists more control over their playing style. This feature is particularly advantageous for those who enjoy expressive playing techniques.


Fluted-Shaft Tuners:

The fluted-shaft tuners offer smooth and precise tuning adjustments, adding to the overall tuning stability of the instrument. This is crucial for maintaining accurate pitch during performances and studio recordings.


Professional-Grade Construction:

Crafted in Fender's American factory, the Professional Jazz Bass exemplifies top-tier construction and attention to detail. The choice of high-quality materials ensures durability and longevity.


Player-Friendly Features:

From the comfortable neck profile to the easily accessible truss rod adjustment wheel, the American Professional Jazz Bass is designed with the player in mind, offering features that enhance the overall playing experience.


Visual Appeal:

Available in various finishes, the Jazz Bass not only delivers on performance but also boasts a visually striking appearance, making it a standout instrument on any stage or in any studio.


Conclusion:

For professional bassists seeking a reliable, versatile, and meticulously crafted instrument, the Fender American Professional Jazz Bass stands as a testament to Fender's legacy of delivering top-tier bass guitars.
