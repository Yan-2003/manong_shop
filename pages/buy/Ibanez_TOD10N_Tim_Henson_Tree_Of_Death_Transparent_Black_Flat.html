The Ibanez TOD10N Tim Henson Tree Of Death in Transparent Black Flat is a signature model designed in collaboration with Tim Henson, the guitarist of the progressive metal band Polyphia. This unique and high-performance guitar is crafted to meet the demands of modern players and features striking aesthetics.


Key Features:



Unique Tree Of Death Inlay:

The guitar features a distinctive "Tree Of Death" fretboard inlay, adding a touch of artistic flair. This intricate and eye-catching inlay design is a signature element of Tim Henson's style and sets this guitar apart visually.


Solid Body Construction:

With a solid body construction, the TOD10N provides a foundation for powerful and sustained tones. The choice of tonewoods contributes to the guitar's overall resonance and sustain, making it suitable for various playing styles.


Transparent Black Flat Finish:

The Transparent Black Flat finish not only showcases the natural beauty of the guitar's wood grain but also adds a modern and sleek aesthetic. The flat finish enhances the guitar's resonance while providing a smooth and comfortable feel.


Wizard III Roasted Maple Neck:

The Wizard III Roasted Maple neck offers a slim and fast-playing profile. Roasted maple provides added stability and resonance while contributing to the guitar's overall playability and responsiveness.


DiMarzio® Fusion Edge Pickups:

Equipped with DiMarzio® Fusion Edge pickups, the TOD10N delivers a high-output and articulate tone. These pickups are well-suited for the dynamic playing styles associated with modern progressive metal and rock genres.


Floating Mono-Rail Bridge:

The floating Mono-Rail bridge enhances string separation and resonance. This design choice allows each string to vibrate independently, minimizing interference and maximizing clarity, making it ideal for intricate playing techniques.


Gotoh® MG-T Locking Machine Heads:

The Gotoh® MG-T locking machine heads provide tuning stability and quick string changes. The locking mechanism ensures precise tuning, even during aggressive playing and heavy use.


Killswitch and Coil-Tap Functionality:

Featuring a killswitch and coil-tap functionality, the TOD10N offers additional sonic options. The killswitch allows for stutter effects, while the coil-tap function provides a single-coil tone for added versatility.


Visual Aesthetics:

With its unique inlay design, transparent finish, and modern features, the Ibanez TOD10N Tim Henson Tree Of Death is a visually striking instrument. It reflects Tim Henson's artistic vision and stands out on stage or in any musical setting.


Conclusion:

Designed for players who appreciate modern aesthetics, versatile tones, and innovative features, the Ibanez TOD10N Tim Henson Tree Of Death Transparent Black Flat is a signature model that combines style and performance. Whether you're playing progressive metal or exploring various genres, this guitar is built to inspire.
